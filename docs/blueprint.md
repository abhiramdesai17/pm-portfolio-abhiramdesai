# **App Name**: PM Connect

## Core Features:

- Work Section: Display detailed case studies and key projects, including 'Mental Health AI Chatbot', 'Picture to Text Project', and 'Credit Card Default Prediction', with problem, solution, role, metrics, and outcomes in a visually appealing format.
- Play Section: Showcase personal projects, hobbies, and creative endeavors to provide a holistic view of the product manager's interests and skills outside of formal work.
- About Me Section: A dedicated area for the PM's professional bio, philosophy, experience highlights, and professional journey.
- Interactive Skill Matrix: Visually represent core product management skills, tools, and proficiency levels, allowing for easy comprehension of expertise.
- Contact & Social Links: Provide clear and accessible links for contact information, LinkedIn, GitHub, and other relevant professional profiles.
- AI Project Storyteller: A generative AI tool that assists users in crafting compelling and concise narrative summaries or bullet points for project descriptions based on raw input, highlighting key achievements.
- Resume Download: Enable visitors to download the user's resume or CV directly from the portfolio website.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional steel blue (#2E5177), chosen to evoke a sense of strategic thinking and reliability.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, subtle blue-grey (#EDF1F4), ensuring excellent readability and a clean, modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: A bright, clear cyan (#4ED0D0), selected for calls to action and to provide a refreshing contrast, symbolizing clarity and innovation.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), for its modern, objective, and highly legible appearance, suitable for showcasing professional content.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist line-art icons that complement the professional tone and enhance navigation without visual clutter.
- Implement a modular grid-based layout for project displays to maintain visual hierarchy and responsiveness, emphasizing content blocks.
- Subtle, smooth hover effects and transitions on interactive elements like project cards or skill indicators to provide an engaging user experience.
